Popular Tours

Sary chelek lake

The lake of extraordinary beauty is hidden in the depths of the western Tien Shan. It has an indescribable beauty, reflecting the blue sky and the green slopes of the mountains.

Sary-Too

Here you can see all the colors that can only exist in nature. Multicolored like a rainbow - terracotta, turquoise, lemon, emerald, sand, black, purple.

Son kul lake

Son-Kul is a precious pearl in an expensive frame of snowy mountains. The untouched beauty of the lake and its surroundings creates a unique picture

Lenin Peak

Rising to the top of Lenin Peak, you feel like you are in the realm of ice and eternal snows. For climbers at the foot of the mountain, in the tract Achyk-Tash, there is a base camp.

Kol-Tor lake

Lake Kol-Tor is a moderate hike to an incredible mountain lake, painted in the most incredible shade of milky aquamarine.

Ala-Archa park

Walk through the mountain valley and dip your hands in the mountain streams. Beautiful views, fresh air and all this - not far from Bishkek.
